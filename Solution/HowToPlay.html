IsWatching: How To Play

Main Menu

Controls

WASD - Movement
Space - Crouch
Mouse - First person camera controls
LMB / RMB - Focus Flashlight

Gameplay

Eyes: Destroy 5 eyes in each environment with your focused flashlight. To destroy them, hold the LMB / RMB and aim at the eyes with the beam center until it is destroyed, or partially destroyed.
IsWatching: Inbetween destroying eye balls, you must avoid the being seen. When the light turns red, avoid being seen by hiding under a bed or table with crouch. Staying in sight for too long has unfortunate consquences...
